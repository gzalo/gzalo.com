This project is a simplified driver for Arduino Pro Mini (atmega328p) to control Futaba M202MD08A VFD displays, used in IBM 41K6814 Point of Sale terminals (20x2 dual VFD displays).

## [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/gzalo/LCD-M202MD08A)
